About Dr. Kayla Yosipovich

Dr. Kayla Yosipovich

PSY.D. | CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGIST

Office Location: Vaughan, Ontario

Dr. Kayla Yosipovich is a registered Clinical Psychologist with the College of Psychologists of Ontario, and a member of the Ontario Psychological Association. She graduated from Ferkauf Graduate School of Psychology with a Doctorate (PsyD) in Clinical Child Psychology and a Masters (MS) in School Psychology.

Dr. Yosipovich has over ten years of experience providing assessment and therapeutic intervention for children and adolescents. Dr. Yosipovich has worked in inpatient outpatient clinical settings, and within the school system. Some of her experience includes her work at North York General Hospital, Toronto District School Board, North Shore-LIJ Hospital, and Leake and Watts Residential Treatment Center.

She treats mood and anxiety disorders, emotional regulation challenges, and interpersonal difficulties, and provides parenting support and school consultation.
