Resources

Helpful Information for Parents and Families

We've compiled a collection of resources to support you and your family. Whether you're preparing for your first visit or looking for additional support, these resources can help guide your journey.

Getting Started

What to Expect

Your first session will focus on understanding your child's needs and building a comfortable therapeutic relationship. Dr. Yosipovich will work with you to develop a personalized treatment plan.

Preparing for Your First Visit

Bring any relevant medical or educational records, a list of current medications, and insurance information if applicable. It's helpful to discuss with your child what to expect in a supportive, age-appropriate way.

Session Length & Frequency

Session length and frequency vary depending on the type of service and individual needs. Initial assessments may take longer than follow-up therapy sessions. Dr. Yosipovich will discuss the recommended schedule during your first visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you work with?

Dr. Yosipovich specializes in working with children and adolescents, providing both assessment and therapeutic services tailored to each developmental stage.

Do you accept insurance?

Many insurance plans cover psychological services. Please contact your insurance provider to verify your coverage and benefits. We can provide receipts for insurance reimbursement.

How long does therapy take?

The length of therapy varies depending on individual needs and treatment goals. Some families see improvement within a few months, while others benefit from longer-term support.

Are parents involved in therapy?

Yes! Dr. Yosipovich is a strong proponent of involving parents in treatment. Parent involvement is essential for lasting change and successful outcomes.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule your appointment.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Please contact us to discuss virtual session options and determine what format would work best for your family's needs.
