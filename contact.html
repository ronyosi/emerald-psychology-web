Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you work with?

Dr. Yosipovich specializes in working with children and adolescents, providing both assessment and therapeutic services.

Do you accept insurance?

Many insurance plans cover psychological services. Please contact your insurance provider to verify your coverage and benefits.

How long is a typical session?

Session length varies depending on the type of service. Initial assessments may take longer than follow-up therapy sessions.

What should I bring to the first appointment?

Please bring any relevant medical or educational records, a list of current medications, and insurance information if applicable.
